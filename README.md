## Course
Critical Thinking, Design Thinking, Leadership and Teamwork (CTDT)

## Task
Task #05 - Inference Mapping

## Topic
Artificial Intelligence in Education: Benefits and Challenges

## Objective
To analyze the impact of Artificial Intelligence in education by identifying observations, causes, effects, solutions, and expected outcomes through inference mapping.

## Problem Statement
The rapid adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in education is transforming learning methods and educational practices. While AI offers several advantages, it also introduces challenges that require responsible usage.

## Observations
- Students frequently use AI tools for assignments and research.
- AI provides quick access to information.
- Educational institutions are adopting AI-powered learning platforms.
- Dependence on technology is increasing.

## Causes
- Easy availability of AI tools.
- Need for faster learning and problem-solving.
- Growth of digital education.
- Increased use of smart devices and internet services.

## Effects

### Positive Effects
- Personalized learning experiences.
- Improved productivity.
- Faster access to educational resources.
- Better support for self-learning.

### Negative Effects
- Overdependence on AI tools.
- Reduced critical thinking skills.
- Risk of plagiarism and academic dishonesty.
- Less independent problem-solving.

## Solutions
- Promote responsible AI usage.
- Establish ethical guidelines for AI use.
- Encourage critical thinking activities.
- Combine AI-assisted learning with practical learning.
- Provide awareness about AI limitations.

## Expected Outcomes
- Balanced use of AI in education.
- Improved learning outcomes.
- Better critical thinking abilities.
- Ethical and responsible technology usage.
- Enhanced educational experience.
